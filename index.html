Learn to play guitar

Curriculum

Teaching all skill levels from a variety of ages. Lesson plans are oriented around what your interests are. I'll provide you with training in skills such as learning chord shapes, scales, arpeggio structures, ear training, transcription, reading chord charts, improvisation, arranging, and live performance.

Location

Guitar lessons are offered from my home studio, located near the mouth of Provo Canyon. Online lessons are available as well through a video conferencing software of your choice.

Schedule

Currently teaching from 3:00pm - 7:00pm, Monday through Thursday.

Pricing

Lessons are $25 for a half-hour timeslot, however, hour time slots are available for $45.
